About the calculator

  • I developed this by comparing it with my phone calculator which is Itel p32. So,i am open to any critism and kudos will be welcome as well.

    • Usage of the calculator for non-itel p32 users

  • Click the figures buttons before clicking the function buttons for any operation.
  • Click the AC button to erase the value completely(though this is not added to the main calculator).
  • Cllick the light-green to operate the scientifc aspects of the calculator.
  • Click the light-green back to close the scientific page.
  • Click the INV button after clicking the light-green to operate with
    • Arc sine(sin`1)
    • Arc cos(cos`1)
    • Arc tan(tan`1)
    • 10x(to get any value raised to the power of 10)
    • ex(to get the exponencial function of any number)
    • !(to get the factorial value of any number)
  • Click the INV button back to display it off immediately after its operation otherwise, it may bring confusion when operating AC & DEL buttons.
